Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

At Secondary NG (accessible from https://secondary.com.ng/), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by our website and PWA (Progressive Web App) and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create accounts to access core educational content. However, we may collect:

  • Log Files: Like most websites, we follow a standard procedure of using log files (IP addresses, browser type, ISP, date/time stamp).
  • Service Worker Data: Our app uses service workers to cache content for offline use on your device.

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Secondary NG uses 'cookies' to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. This is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content.

3. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Secondary NG's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party servers (such as Google Analytics or AdSense) for more detailed information.

4. Data Protection Rights (GDPR/NDPR)

We want to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access, rectify, or erase your data.
  • The right to request that we restrict the processing of your data.

5. Data Deletion

Since we do not store personal user accounts, there is no personal data on our servers to delete. If you wish to clear usage data, you can do so by clearing your browser cache or uninstalling the PWA.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contact@secondary.com.ng.
