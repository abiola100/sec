JUDO (A COMBAT SPORT)

Judo is a martial art that focuses on using balance, skill, and techniques to defeat an opponent rather than strength.



Origin:

Judo originated in Japan and was created by Dr. Jigoro Kano in 1882.



Basic Skills in judo


  1. Ukemi - break-fall techniques

  2. Osoto Gari - outer leg sweep

  3. Ippon Seoi Nage - shoulder throw



Equipment


  • Judo uniform (judogi)

  • Belt to show rank

  • Mat (tatami) to protect during falls



Rules and Regulations


  • No hitting or kickin

  • Respect your opponent

  • Matches begin and end with a bow

  • Points are awarded for clean throws or holds
