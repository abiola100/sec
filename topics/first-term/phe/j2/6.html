Field Events - Throws and Jumps

Field events are sports that involve throwing or jumping instead of running.



Types of Throws


  1. Shot Put - throwing a heavy ball (put) for distance

  2. Discus - spinning and throwing a round disc

  3. Javelin - throwing a spear-like stick

Types of Jumps


  1. Long Jump - jump for distance into a sandpit

  2. High Jump - jump over a horizontal bar

  3. Triple Jump - includes hop, step, and jump



Jumping Rules:


  • No overstepping the takeoff board

  • Land properly

  • Must not knock down the crossbar
