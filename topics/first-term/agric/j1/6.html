CLASSIFICATION OF FOOD AND THEIR SOURCE
CLASSES OF FOOD:
  1. Carbohydrates : Carbohydrate are macronutrients that provide the body with energy.
    2. source of Carbohydrates
    • Sugar : Fruit, honey, table sugar.

    • Starch : Grains(wheat,rice),potatoes,yam,cassava.

    • Fiber : whole grains,fruits, Vegetable,nuts,seeds, and legumes.

  2. Protein : These are macronutrients essential for building and reparing tissues, as well as for various bodyly functions.
    Common source of Protein
    • Animal source : Meat(beef, poultry,pork), fish,eggs, diary products(milk, cheese, yogurt).
    • Plant source : Legumes (beans,lentils),nuts, seeds,tofu etc

  3. Fat : these are macronutrients that provide energy, support cell growth, and help body absorb certain vitamings. common sources of fats include butter, palm oil, olive oil, coconut oil,avocado,nuts,seed fatty fish etc

  4. vitamings : vitamings are essential micronutrients that the body required in samll amount to function properly.
    • Vitamin A: this important for vision, immune function and skin health. source include, diary product, carrot,Vegetable, orange etc.
    • Vitamin D: help regulate calcuim and phosphorus absorbtion, it is very important for bone health. source milk, sunlight exposure etc.
    • Vitamin E and Vitamin K

  5. Miniral : This are inorganic substances that are essential for various physiological functions in the body.

  6. Water : water is important for hydration, nutrient transport. this is found in beverages(water,tea,coffee, juice)
