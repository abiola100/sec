Forms of agricultural activities in the community

  1. crop farming :   This is a form of farming that involve planting of crops for human or animal consumption. A crop is a plant that can be grown and harvested extensively for business purspose or for family or personal sustenance. example: maize, yam,cassava, sweet potato etc.

  2. Live-stock farming :   This is a form of agriculture which involve raising of domesticated animal in an agricultural setting in order to produce products for consumption such as egg, meat, milk, leather, wool etc.

  3. Fishery :   This refers to a brach of agriculture which deals with rearing or harvesting of fish and other aquatic life or more for commercial or personal consumption.

  4. Heliculture :   this is otherwise known as snail farming, it is the process of raising land snail, majorly for human consumption or cosmetic use .

  5. Horticulture :   this can be define as a the branch of agriculture that deals with garden crops , generally fruit, vegetables , and ornamental plants. Horticulture is derived from latin word "hortus" means garden, and "colere" means cultivate.

  6. Apiculture :   Otherwise known as Beekeeping, this is the branch of agriculture which deals with rearing and maintenance of bee colonies, commonly in artificial beehive. The bees are rasied for commercial purpose.
