SOURCE DOCUMENTS


  1. Sales Invoice : Issued based on a sales order, detailing sold products and amount due from customers.

  2. Purchase Invoice : Received from a purchase order, detailing purchased products and amount owed to suppliers.

  3. Return Inward : Documented through a return request, detailing returned goods from customers.

  4. Return Outward : Issued via return authorization, detailing goods returned to suppliers.

  5. Invoices :Documents issued by a business to its customers, detailing products or services provided and the amount due.

  6. Receipts :Proof of payment received from customers for goods or services.

  7. Bank Statements :Monthly records from the bank showing all transactions in the business account.

  8. Purchase Orders :Documents sent by a business to suppliers, detailing the items or services requested.

  9. Bills and Statements :Invoices received from suppliers, utilities, and other service providers that need to be paid.

  10. Payroll Records :Documents related to employee compensation, including wages, taxes, and deductions.
