MAINTENANCE OF FARM STRUCTURES AND BUILDING

  1. Regular Inspection and Repairs: Inspect all structures for damage and wear. Fix any issues promptly to prevent further deterioration.

  2. Pest Control: Seal gaps and entry points to keep pests out. Use traps and repellents as needed.

  3. Roof and Gutter Maintenance :Check for and repair roof damage. Clean gutters to prevent water damage.

  4. Electrical and Plumbing Maintenance :Inspect electrical wiring and plumbing for issues. Repair or replace damaged components promptly.

  5. Ventilation Maintenance :Ensure ventilation systems are clean and functioning. Maintain airflow to prevent mold and humidity problems.

  6. Foundation and Structural Support :Inspect the foundation and support beams for cracks or shifts. Repair any damage to maintain structural integrity.

  7. Painting and Surface Treatment :Repaint or treat surfaces to protect against weather and wear. This helps prevent rust, rot, and other damage.

  8. Door and Window Maintenance :Check doors and windows for proper sealing and function. Repair or replace broken latches, hinges, and panes.

  9. Fence and Gate Upkeep :Inspect fences and gates for damage or wear. Repair broken sections and ensure gates are functional.

  10. Storage and Organization :Keep storage areas clean and organized. Regularly check for signs of damage or pests in stored equipment and materials.
