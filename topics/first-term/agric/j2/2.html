MAINTENANCE OF FARM STRUCTURES AND BUILDING




  1. Inspect all structures for damage and wear. Fix any issues promptly to prevent further deterioration.

  2. Seal gaps and entry points to keep pests out. Use traps and repellents as needed.

  3. Check for and repair roof damage. Clean gutters to prevent water damage.

  4. Inspect electrical wiring and plumbing for issues. Repair or replace damaged components promptly.

  5. Ensure ventilation systems are clean and functioning. Maintain airflow to prevent mold and humidity problems.

  6. Inspect the foundation and support beams for cracks or shifts. Repair any damage to maintain structural integrity.

  7. Repaint or treat surfaces to protect against weather and wear. This helps prevent rust, rot, and other damage.

  8. Check doors and windows for proper sealing and function. Repair or replace broken latches, hinges, and panes.

  9. Inspect fences and gates for damage or wear. Repair broken sections and ensure gates are functional.

  10. Keep storage areas clean and organized. Regularly check for signs of damage or pests in stored equipment and materials.
