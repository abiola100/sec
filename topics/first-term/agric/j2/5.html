EFFECT OF TIMELY HARVESTING VERSUS LATE HARVESTING


Timely haversting:
  1. Quality : Crops are at their peak, ensuring optimal taste, texture, and nutritional value.

  2. Yield : Higher yield as crops are harvested before any damage or loss.

  3. Pest/Disease Control : Reduces the risk of pests and diseases.

  4. Market Value : Higher market prices due to better quality and freshness.

  5. Storage : Easier to store and preserve since crops are harvested at the right time.





Late Harvesting:
  1. Quality : Deterioration in taste, texture, and nutritional value..

  2. Yield : Reduced yield due to over-ripeness, spoilage, or pest damage.

  3. Pest/Disease Control: Increased risk of pests and diseases.

  4. Market Value : Lower prices due to reduced quality and appearance.

  5. Labor Costs : Potentially higher labor costs due to the need for more careful handling of overripe crops.
