EFFECT OF TIMELY HARVESTING VERSUS LATE HARVESTING



effect of timely haversting:

  1. Crops are at their peak, ensuring optimal taste, texture, and nutritional value.

  2. Higher yield as crops are harvested before any damage or loss.

  3. Reduces the risk of pests and diseases.

  4. Higher market prices due to better quality and freshness.

  5. Easier to store and preserve since crops are harvested at the right time.






Effect late harvesting:

  1. Deterioration in taste, texture, and nutritional value..

  2. Reduced yield due to over-ripeness, spoilage, or pest damage.

  3. Increased risk of pests and diseases.

  4. Lower prices due to reduced quality and appearance.

  5. Potentially higher labor costs due to the need for more careful handling of overripe crops.
