FAMILY TRAITS

Family traits refer to physical, genetic, and behavioral characteristics passed from one generation to another within a family.





TYPES OF FAMILY TRAITS
  1. Physical Traits
    • Eye color
    • Hair color and texture
    • Skin tone
    • Height
    • Facial features (e.g., shape of the nose, chin, etc.)

  2. Genetic Traits
    • Blood type
    • Inherited diseases or conditions (e.g., cystic fibrosis, sickle cell anemia, diabetes)
    • Metabolism rate

  3. Behavioral and Personality Traits
    1. Temperament (e.g., introversion vs. extroversion)
    2. Talents and skills (e.g., musical ability, athleticism)
    3. Learning abilities and preferences

  4. Cultural and social traits (e.g., traditions, values)


  5. Intellectual interests and hobbies


  6. Speech patterns and accents





DOMINANT AND RECESSIVE TRAITS
Domoinant trait

What is dominant traits ?

A dominant trait can be define as the kind of trait that appears more prominently in an individual when inherited from at least one parent.


Examples:Brown eyes, Widow's peak (V-shaped hairline),Dimples etc.



Recessive trait

What is Recessive trait ?

A recessive trait van be define as the one that is expressed only when inherited from both parents, who must each contribute a recessive allele.

Examples:Blue eyes, Straight hairline, No dimples etc.
