CONTROL MEASURE ON OZONE LAYER DEPLETION
  1. A global agreement to stop using harmful chemicals that damage the ozone layer, like CFCs and halons.

  2. Strict rules that ban the production and use of ozone-depleting substances in products and industries.

  3. Finding and using new substances that don't harm the ozone layer, like HFCs instead of CFCs.

  4. Teaching everyone about why it's important to protect the ozone layer and use safe alternatives.

  5. Making sure companies and countries follow the rules and don't use harmful chemicals.

  6. Countries around the world working together to solve the problem of ozone layer damage.
