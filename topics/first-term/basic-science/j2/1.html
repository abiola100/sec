HABITAT


habitat can be define as the natural environment in which a particular species of plant, animal, or other organism lives and thrives.


Examples :
  1. Tropical Rainforest: Cross River Rainforest in southeastern Nigeria.

  2. Temperate Forest: Montane forests on the Mambilla Plateau in northeastern Nigeria.

  3. Desert: Sahel region in the northeastern part of Nigeria.

  4. Grassland (Savanna): Savanna region in the Middle Belt of Nigeria.

  5. Grassland (Prairie): Derived savanna in the southwestern part of Nigeria.

  6. Wetland: Niger Delta wetlands in southern Nigeria.

  7. Ocean (Coral Reef): Coral reefs near the Bonny Island in the Gulf of Guinea.

  8. Ocean (Deep Sea): Deep-sea areas off the Nigerian coast in the Atlantic Ocean.

  9. River: Niger River flowing through Nigeria.

  10. Mountain: Obudu Plateau in southeastern Nigeria.

  11. Urban Area: Lagos in southwestern Nigeria.
