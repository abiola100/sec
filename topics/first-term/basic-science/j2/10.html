SAFETY MEASURE WHEN USING CHEMICALS


  1. Use gloves, goggles, lab coats, aprons, and respirators as needed.

  2. Read Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) or Safety Data Sheets (SDS).

  3. Ensure proper training on handling, storage, and disposal.

  4. Use fume hoods or well-ventilated areas.

  5. Properly label all chemical containers.

  6. Avoid eating or drinking in chemical areas.

  7. Store chemicals by hazard classification and compatibility.

  8. Use lockable cabinets for hazardous chemicals.

  9. Keep spill kits and neutralizing agents available.

  10. Know the location and use of safety showers, eye wash stations, fire extinguishers, and first aid kits.
