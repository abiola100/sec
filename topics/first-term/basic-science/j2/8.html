CLASSIFICATION OF CHEMICALS BASED ON USE
  1. Agricultural Chemicals, These include:

    • Pesticides: Insecticides, herbicides, fungicides.

    • Fertilizers: Nitrogen-based, phosphorus-based, potassium-based.






  2. Industrial Chemicals:
    • Solvents: Acetone, methanol, toluene.

    • Lubricants: Motor oils, grease.

    • Plastics and Polymers: Polyethylene, polypropylene, PVC.

    • Dyes and Pigments: Aniline dyes, titanium dioxide.




  3. Household Chemicals:
    • Cleaning Agents: Detergents, bleach, ammonia-based cleaners.

    • Personal Care Products: Shampoo, toothpaste, deodorants.

    • Disinfectants: Alcohol-based, chlorine-based.




  4. Pharmaceuticals:
    • Prescription Drugs: Antibiotics, antidepressants, antipsychotics.

    • Over-the-counter Drugs: Pain relievers, cough syrups, antacids.




  5. Food Additives:
    • Preservatives: Sodium benzoate, potassium sorbate.

    • Flavor Enhancers: Monosodium glutamate (MSG).

    • Color Additives: Tartrazine, caramel color.

    • Nutritional Additives: Vitamins, minerals.




  6. Construction Chemicals: examples,Cement and Concrete Additives,Sealants and Coatings etc.




  7. Electronic Chemicals:
    • Semiconductor Chemicals: Silicon wafers, photoresists.

    • Battery Chemicals: Lithium salts, sulfuric acid.




  8. Textile Chemicals:
    • Dyes: Reactive dyes, disperse dyes.

    • Finishing Agents: Softening agents, anti-static agents.
