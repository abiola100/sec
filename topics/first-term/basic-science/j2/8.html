CLASSIFICATION OF CHEMICALS BASED ON USE




  1. Agricultural Chemicals: Used for farming, such as fertilizers and pesticides.




  2. Industrial Chemicals: Used in factories, like acids, bases, and solvents.






  3. Household Chemicals: Used at home, like detergents, bleach, and cleaning agents.






  4. Medicinal Chemicals: Used for health, like drugs, vaccines, and antibiotics.






  5. Food Chemicals: Used in food, like preservatives, flavorings, and additives.






  6. Construction Chemicals: Used in building, such as cement additives, paints, and adhesives.






  7. Electronic Chemicals: Used in making electronics, like silicon, solder, and conductive materials.






  8. Textile Chemicals: Used in clothing and fabric, like dyes, bleaches, and softeners.
