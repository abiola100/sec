CLASSIFICATION OF CHEMICALS BASES HAZARDOUS NATURE
  1. Flammable Chemicals: These are types of chemicals that are Easily ignitable and can cause fires or explosions.
    Examples: Ethanol, Acetone, Benzene etc.

  2. Toxic Chemicals: These are types of chemicals that are Harmful or fatal if ingested, inhaled, or absorbed through the skin.
    Examples: Cyanide, Mercury, Arsenic.

  3. Corrosive Chemicals:These are types of chemicals that Can cause severe damage to living tissues and materials on contact.
    Examples: Hydrochloric acid, Sodium hydroxide, Sulfuric acid.

  4. Oxidizing Chemicals:These are types of chemicals that Can cause or enhance the combustion of other materials.
    Examples: Hydrogen peroxide, Potassium permanganate, Nitric acid.

  5. Explosive Chemicals:These are types of chemicals that Can detonate or explode under certain conditions.
    Examples: TNT (Trinitrotoluene), Ammonium nitrate, Nitroglycerin.

  6. rritants:These are types of chemicals that can Cause inflammation and irritation to the skin, eyes, or respiratory system.
    Examples: Ammonia, Formaldehyde, Chlorine gas.

  7. Carcinogenic Chemicals:These are types of chemicals that are Known to cause cancer in humans or animals.
    Examples: Asbestos, Benzene, Formaldehyde.

  8. Mutagenic Chemicals: These are types of chemicals that Can cause genetic mutations in living organisms.
    Examples: Ethidium bromide, Nitrous acid, Formaldehyde.

  9. Teratogenic Chemicals: Can cause birth defects and developmental malformations .
    Examples: Thalidomide, Lead compounds, Alcohol.

  10. Sensitizing Chemicals: Can cause allergic reactions after repeated exposure.
    Examples: Nickel compounds, Chromium compounds, Isocyanates.

  11. Radioactive Chemicals: Emit radiation that can cause damage to living tissues. Examples: Uranium, Plutonium, Radon.
