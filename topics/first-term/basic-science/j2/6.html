HUMAN DEVELOPMENT CHANGES

Human developmen changes based on duration
  1. Temporary Development Changes: can be define as Short-term adjustments or developments that can change frequently and may not have lasting effects.like starting a new school,or temporary changes in daily routines etc.

  2. Permanent Development Changes : can be define as the Long-term or lifelong changes that have enduring effects on an individual’s abilities, behaviors, or personality.Examples: Acquisition of language,formation of long-term habits etc.
