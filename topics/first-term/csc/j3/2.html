COMPUTER PROFESSIONALS BODIES


Computer professional bodies are official organizations or associations formed by computer professionals. They help guide, control, and support people who work in computer-related fields.



Importance of Computer Professional Bodies


  1. Computer professional bodies promote good ethics (right behavior) among computer professionals.

  2. they Organize training, workshops, and seminars for computer professionals.

  3. Computer professional bodies issue certificates to qualified computer experts.

  4. They protect the interest of their members.

  5. Computer professional bodies set standards for the computer profession.

  6. They advise the government on computer matters.

  7. Computer professional bodies help members stay up-to-date with the latest technology.

Function of Information Technology Association of Nigeria (ITAN)


  1. Promotes the growth and development of information technology in Nigeria.

  2. Encourages the use of locally made IT products and solutions.

  3. Protects the interests of IT companies and professionals in Nigeria.

  4. Organizes workshops, seminars, and training for IT professionals.

  5. Works with the government to make good IT policies.

  6. Helps members keep up with the latest technology trends.



Function of Institute for the Management of Information Systems (IMIS)



  1. They Provides training and professional qualifications in managing computer and information systems.

  2. They Helps members learn how to manage IT systems in organizations.

  3. IMIS Encourages high standards in the use and management of computer systems.

  4. They Promotes good ethics (right behavior) among information system managers.

  5. They Supports research and development in information systems.




Function of The Nigeria Internet Group (NIG )


  1. The Nigeria Internet Group Promotes safe, responsible, and widespread use of the internet in Nigeria.

  2. The Nigeria Internet Group Organizes seminars and conferences on internet development.

  3. The Nigeria Internet Group Works to expand access to the internet across Nigeria.

  4. The Nigeria Internet Group Provides advice to government and organizations on internet-related issues.

  5. The Nigeria Internet Group Encourages internet security and privacy among users.

  6. The Nigeria Internet Group Supports development of local internet content and services.



function of Computer Professionals (Registration Council of Nigeria)


  1. Registers qualified computer professionals in Nigeria.

  2. They Controls and regulates the practice of computer professionals in Nigeria.

  3. They Ensures that only registered (certified) people work as computer professionals.

  4. They Sets standards and rules for the computer profession in Nigeria.

  5. Punishes members who break the rules of the profession.

  6. Advises the government on computer-related matters.



Nigeria Computer Society (NCS)


  1. Nigeria Computer Society Promotes the use and development of Information Technology (IT) in Nigeria.

  2. They Brings together computer professionals, companies, and students under one body.

  3. they Organizes conferences, workshops, and seminars to train members.

  4. They Supports research and innovation in IT.

  5. NCS Encourages the use of computers in schools, offices, and industries.

  6. Nigeria Computer Society Advises government on policies related to IT development.
