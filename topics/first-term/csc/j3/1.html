COMPUTER PROFESSIONALS

A computer professional is a person who has undergone training in computer science or information technology and is skilled in working with computers.

They are experts who design, build, program, repair, maintain, or manage computer systems and software.



Examples of Computer Professionals


  1. Computer Engineer – They designs and builds computer hardware (physical parts of the computer).

  2. Computer Programmer / Software Developer – They writes instructions (code) for computers to perform tasks.

  3. System Analyst – Studies computer systems and finds ways to improve them.

  4. Network Administrator / Network Engineer – They sets up and manages computer networks.

  5. Database Administrator (DBA) – They organizes and manages data using databases.

  6. Web Designer / Web Developer – They creates and manages websites.

  7. IT Support / Computer Technician – They repairs computers and helps solve computer problems.

  8. Cybersecurity Expert – They protects computer systems from viruses and hackers.


Qualities of a Computer Professional


  1. A good computer professional should Be hardworking and dedicated

  2. A good computer professional should Be honest and trustworthy

  3. A good computer professional should Have good communication skills

  4. A good computer professional should Be creative and innovative

  5. A good computer professional should Pay attention to details

  6. A good computer professional should Be ready to learn new things because technology changes often

  7. A good computer professional should Work well alone or as part of a team


Importance of Computer Professionals


  1. Computer professionals help in the design, building, and repair of computers

  2. They write software code that make computers useful

  3. They protect computers from viruses and hackers

  4. they make sure that computer system is working properly

  5. They train other people on how to use computers

  6. They build and manage websites that we use every day


Differences Between Computer Users and Computer Professionals



Computer User Computer Professional
1 Uses the computer to do work like typing, browsing, playing games, etc. Designs, builds, repairs, or programs the computer or its software
2 Needs little or no computer training Needs special training and skills in computer science
3 Works with ready-made programs Creates or manages computer programs and systems
