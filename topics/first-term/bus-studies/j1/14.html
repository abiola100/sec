ACTIVITIES WHICH AID COMMERCE/TRADE




  • Transport and Logistics: Moving goods from producers to consumers efficiently.

  • Warehousing: Storing goods safely until they are needed.

  • Banking and Finance: Providing financial services like loans, payments, and investments.

  • Advertising and Marketing: Promoting products and services to attract customers.

  • Insurance: Protecting businesses against risks and losses.

  • Communication: Ensuring effective exchange of information between businesses and customers.

  • Sales and Distribution: Getting products to customers through various sales channels.

  • Customer Service: Helping customers with their needs and solving their problems.

  • E-commerce Platforms: Providing online spaces for buying and selling goods and services.

  • Legal Services: Ensuring businesses comply with laws and regulations.

  • Accounting and Bookkeeping: Managing financial records and ensuring accurate financial reporting.

  • Market Research: Understanding consumer needs and market trends to make better business decisions.

  • Packaging: Protecting goods and making them attractive to consumers.

  • Quality Control: Ensuring products meet certain standards before they reach customers.
