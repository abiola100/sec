OFFICE STAFF


Office staff refers to the group of employees working within an office setting who perform various administrative, clerical, managerial, and support tasks essential for the operation of an organization.

They play a crucial role in ensuring the smooth functioning and efficiency of office activities, contributing to the overall productivity and success of the business.
