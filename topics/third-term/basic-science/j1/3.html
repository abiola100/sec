FORCE

Force : can be define as a push or pull on an object that can make it move, stop, or change direction.


TYPES OF FORCE

  1. Gravitational Force: Pulls objects toward each other, like gravity pulling us to the ground.

  2. Frictional Force: Slows down objects when they slide or roll over something.

  3. Normal Force: The support force from a surface holding up an object.

  4. Applied Force: A push or pull from a person or another object.

  5. Tension Force: The force in a string or rope when it's pulled tight.

  6. Elastic Force: The force in a stretched or compressed object, like a spring.

  7. Electromagnetic Force: The force between charged objects, involving both electric and magnetic effects.

  8. Nuclear Force: The force that holds the particles in the nucleus of an atom together.
