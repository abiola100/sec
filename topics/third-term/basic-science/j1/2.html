ENERGY AND SOCIETY

  Understanding the relationship between energy and society is crucial for sustainable development and ensuring a balance between economic growth, social well-being, and environmental protection.





IMPACT OF ENERGY ONTHE SOCIETY
  1. Economic Growth: Reliable energy helps businesses grow and creates jobs.

  2. Quality of Life: Energy powers homes, schools, and hospitals, making life better.

  3. Social Equity: Fair energy access reduces poverty and helps everyone get basic services.

  4. Environmental Impact: The way we use energy affects air quality and the climate.

  5. National Security: Having our own energy sources makes a country safer.

  6. Technological Innovation: New energy technologies lead to new inventions and improvements.

  7. Public Health: Clean energy reduces pollution and health problems.

  8. Cultural Development: Energy access supports cultural activities and keeps us connected.
