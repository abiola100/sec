USES OF CRUDE OIL AND PETROCHEMICALS






USES OF CRUDE OIL


  1. Gasoline: Fuel for cars and light trucks.

  2. Diesel: Fuel for trucks, buses, trains, and some cars.

  3. Jet Fuel: Fuel for aircraft.

  4. Heating Oil: Used for residential and commercial heating.

  5. Bitumen/Asphalt: Used for road construction and roofing.

  6. Lubricants: Reduce friction in engines and machinery.




USES OF PETROCHEMICALS


  1. Plastics: Used in packaging, containers, and various products.

  2. Synthetic Rubber: Used in tires, footwear, and industrial products.

  3. Solvents: Used in paints, coatings, and cleaning products.

  4. Fertilizers: Essential for agriculture to enhance soil nutrients.

  5. Pharmaceuticals: Used in the production of drugs and medical devices.

  6. Synthetic Fibers: Used in textiles, such as polyester and nylon.
