IMPORTANCE OF SKILL ACQUISITION

  1. Employability: Enhances job prospects and career opportunities.

  2. Adaptability: Enables individuals to adapt to changing job markets and technologies.

  3. Economic Growth: Contributes to national productivity and economic development.

  4. Personal Development: Promotes self-improvement and confidence.

  5. Innovation: Drives creativity and the development of new solutions.

  6. Competitiveness: Helps individuals and businesses stay competitive.

  7. Job Satisfaction: Leads to higher job satisfaction and career fulfillment.

  8. Lifelong Learning: Encourages continuous learning and growth.

  9. Problem-Solving: Improves the ability to tackle complex challenges.

  10. Social Mobility: Provides opportunities for upward social mobility.
