REASON FOR SKILL ACQUISITION
  1. Getting a Job: New skills make it easier to find and keep a job.

  2. Career Growth: Skills help you move up in your career and earn more money.

  3. Flexibility: Learning different skills allows you to switch jobs or adapt to changes in the job market.

  4. Creativity: New skills can spark new ideas and solutions.

  5. Personal Development: Gaining skills boosts your confidence and gives you a sense of accomplishment.

  6. Economic Growth: A skilled workforce helps the economy grow.

  7. Problem-Solving: Skills improve your ability to solve problems in work and life.

  8. Starting a Business : Skills can help you start and run your own business.
