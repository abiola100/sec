ETHICAL ISSUE IN SCIENCE AND DEVELOPMENT

Ethical issues in science and development are problems about what is right or wrong that come up when doing scientific research or developing new technologies.



RIGHT AND WRONG APPLICATION OF SCIENCE
Right Application of Science:
  1. Improving Health: Using science to develop medicines and treatments that save lives.

  2. Environmental Protection: Creating sustainable technologies that protect the environment.

  3. Advancing Knowledge: Conducting research that leads to positive innovations.

  4. Ethical Research Practices: Ensuring research is conducted with proper consent and care.

  5. Enhancing Education: Using technology to improve learning and access to education.

Wrong Application of Science:
  1. Causing Harm: Developing technologies that harm people or the environment.

  2. Exploiting Vulnerable Populations: Conducting research that exploits or harms vulnerable groups.

  3. Environmental Damage: Ignoring environmental impact, leading to pollution.

  4. Misinformation and Dishonesty: Falsifying research results or spreading misinformation.

  5. Invasion of Privacy: Using technology to collect and misuse personal data without consent.
