CARE OF THE COMPUTER




You can prolong the lifespan of your computer and ensure it operates efficiently if important care practices are beign followed:.


  1. Regular Cleaning: Keep the keyboard, screen, and vents free from dust and dirt.

  2. Ventilation: Ensure proper airflow to prevent overheating.

  3. Safe Handling: Handle the computer carefully to avoid physical damage.

  4. Antivirus Protection: Install and update antivirus software to guard against malware.

  5. Regular Backups: Backup important files regularly to prevent data loss.

  6. Software Updates: Keep the operating system and software up to date for optimal performance and security.

  7. Disk Cleanup: Regularly clear out unnecessary files and programs to keep the system running efficiently.



ESSENTIAL ITEMS USED FOR THE CARE OF COMPUTER


  1. Microfiber Cloth: For cleaning the screen and surfaces without scratching.

  2. Compressed Air Canister: To blow dust out of the keyboard and vents.

  3. Cleaning Solution: A mild, alcohol-free solution for cleaning screens and surfaces.

  4. Soft Brush: To gently remove dust and debris from the keyboard and components.

  5. External Hard Drive or Cloud Storage: For regular backups to protect important data.

  6. Antivirus Software: To protect the computer from malware and viruses.
