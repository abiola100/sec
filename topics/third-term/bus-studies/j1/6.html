INTRODUCTION TO KEYBOARDING




Meaning of keyboarding :

The keyboarding can be define as a process of entering information using an electronic device with keys as input means, which interacts with the end-user.




IMPORTANCE OF KEYBOARDING


  1. Good keyboarding skills allow you to type faster and more accurately, increasing productivity in work.

  2. chats or social media makes keyboarding very important for effective communications.

  3. Many jobs require computer use and strong keyboarding skills, especially in IT.

  4. These skills allow one to easily navigate within various software applications thereby enabling them perform a lot of computer associated assignments.

  5. Proper techniques in keyboarding will aid in prevention of strain and pain as they promote correct postures as well as you position your hands rightly.


CORRECT SITTING POSTURE FOR KEYBOARDING


  1. Chair Position: You should be seated with your back straight, knees bent at 90 degrees and feet flat against the floor.

  2. Arm and Hand Position: Position the forearms parallel to the ground, keeping them at 90 degrees along with straight wrist avoiding contact with the desk.

  3. Keyboard and Monitor Placement: Make sure that the keyboard is within reach at all times while placing the monitor at eye level which is about an armâ€™s length distance.

  4. Body Alignment: Always relax your shoulders while keeping the upper arms close to your body in order for head, neck and trunk to remain in a line.

  5. Breaks and Movement: Take short breaks regularly to stand up, stretch or even walk about so that you do not have stiffness and strain.
