BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES




A business opportunity is a chance to start or expand a business by offering a product or service that meets a need in the market.



TYPES OF BUSINESS OPPORTUNITY
  1. Local Opportunities: These focus on serving specific communities or regions within Nigeria. For example, opening a small restaurant in Ibadan or a local fashion boutique in Enugu.

  2. National Opportunities: These involve reaching customers across Nigeria. For instance, launching a chain of supermarkets that operates in multiple cities like Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt or a nationwide mobile phone service provider.
