AN ENTREPRENEUR


An entrepreneur is someone who starts and runs a business, taking on financial risks in the hope of making a profit.



PERSONAL QUALITIES OF AN ENTREPRENEUR


  1. They come up with new ideas and find creative solutions to problems.

  2. They handle setbacks well and keep pushing forward despite challenges.

  3. They are willing to take calculated risks to pursue opportunities.

  4. They have a strong Passion and enthusiasm for their business.

  5. They make quick and effective decisions, even under pressure.
