PART OF PAINT ENVIRONMENT


The Paint environment is made up of different parts that help you draw and work with pictures. is made up of different parts that help you draw and work with pictures.


  1. Title Bar : Shows the name of your picture and the Paint program. It has buttons to close, make bigger, or make smaller.

  2. Menu Bar : Has words like File, Edit, View, Image, Colors, and Help. You click them to see more commands.

  3. Tool Box / Tools Group :- Has tools like pencil, brush, eraser, and shape tools for drawing and editing your picture.

  4. Color Box / Color Palette :- Has many colors. You choose the color you want for drawing or filling shapes.

  5. Drawing Area / Work Area / Canvas :- The big white space where you draw and color.

  6. Status Bar :- Shows small information about your picture, like where your mouse is on the screen.

  7. Scroll Bars :- Help you move the drawing up, down, left, or right if it is too big for the screen.



FEATURES OF PAINT ENVIRONMENT


  1. The Paint environment has easy-to-use tools like pencil, brush, eraser, fill tool, and shapes.

  2. It provides a color palette with many colors to choose from.

  3. It has a large drawing area where you can create your picture.

  4. The menu bar contains commands like File, Edit, View to work with your drawing.

  5. It allows drawing of shapes and straight lines easily.

  6. It has a zoom or magnifier feature to make the picture bigger or smaller.

  7. You can undo and redo actions to correct mistakes.

  8. It allows you to save and print your drawing.
