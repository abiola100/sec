INTERNET


Internet can be define as a large network that connects millions of computers all over the world. It allows people to share information, communicate


Features of the Internet


  1. It connects computers and devices across the world.

  2. It works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

  3. It allows people to communicate quickly.

  4. It provides information on almost any topic.

  5. It can be used for business, learning, fun, and more.

  6. It is made up of many networks linked together.


Things You Can Do on the Internet


  1. Send and receive emails

  2. Chat with friends using social media (e.g., WhatsApp, Facebook)

  3. Watch videos on YouTube

  4. Read news and articles

  5. Do online shopping

  6. Play online games


Requirements for Using the Internet


  1. A computer, phone, or tablet

  2. An Internet connection (from service providers like MTN, GLO, Airtel, 9mobile)

  3. A web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, Safari)


Benefits of the Internet


  1. Makes communication fast and easy

  2. Helps in learning and research

  3. Allows online banking and shopping

  4. Connects people globally
  5. Provides entertainment (music, movies, games)


Dangers of the Internet


  1. Exposure to bad or fake information

  2. Risk of being scammed

  3. Addiction to social media or games

  4. Cyberbullying


Safety Rules When Using the Internet


  1. Do not share your password with anyone.

  2. Avoid clicking on strange links.

  3. Be polite to others online.

  4. Tell an adult if you see anything bad or scary.

  5. Do not give personal information (like your home address) to strangers.
