USES OF FISH AND FISH PRODUCT
  1. Human Consumption: Fish are eaten fresh, canned, or smoked, providing a rich source of protein and omega-3 fatty acids.

  2. Fish Oil: Used as a dietary supplement for heart health and inflammation reduction.

  3. Fish Meal: High-protein animal feed for livestock and aquaculture.

  4. Fish Sauce: A condiment in Asian cuisines, adding umami flavor to dishes.

  5. Fish Fertilizer: Natural fertilizer for plants, enhancing growth in organic farming.

  6. Fish Collagen: Used in cosmetics and pharmaceuticals for skin elasticity and anti-aging benefits.

  7. Fish Leather : Fashion accessories like shoes and bags made from durable fish skin.

  8. Fish Gelatin : Gelling agent in food and pharmaceuticals.

  9. Aquarium Fish : Kept as pets in home aquariums.

  10. Bioremediation : Fish help clean polluted water by controlling algae and improving water quality.
