ANIMAL PEST AND DISEAS CONTROL




What is animal pest?

Animal pests are organisms, that infest and harm farm animals by causing irritation, spreading diseases, and sometimes directly damaging their health and productivity. such as insects (flies, mites), arachnids (ticks), or rodents.



Animal diseases

Animal diseases are illnesses or disorders caused by pathogens such as bacteria, viruses, fungi, and parasites, or by non-infectious factors like nutritional deficiencies and genetic conditions, affecting the health, productivity, and well-being of farm animals.



METHOD OF PREVENTION AND CONTROL OF ANIMAL PEST AND DISEASES


  1. Routine examinations to catch early signs of pests or diseases.

  2. Using of Preventive vaccines to protect animals from common diseases.

  3. Keeping animal living areas clean to reduce the risk of pests and infections.

  4. Isolating new or sick animals to prevent the spread of diseases.

  5. Using methods like chemical treatments, biological controls, and habitat management to control pests effectively.

  6. Ensuring animals receive a balanced diet to boost their immune systems and overall health.

  7. Using physical traps and barriers to keep pests away from animals.

  8. Administering treatments to control internal parasites.

  9. Keeping detailed records of animal health, treatments, and pest occurrences to identify patterns and improve management.

  10. Educating farm workers on recognizing and managing pests and diseases to ensure early detection and proper response.
