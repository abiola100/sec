FISHERY


what is fishery

  A fishery Fishery is the organized management and harvesting of fish, involving breeding, conservation, and sustainable resource use.



IMPORTANCE OF FISHERY
  1. Provides a major source of protein and nutrition.

  2. Supports livelihoods for millions of people worldwide.

  3. Contributes to economic growth and foreign exchange earnings.

  4. Enhances food security by supplying affordable food.

  5. Promotes sustainable use of aquatic resources.

  6. Supports recreational activities and tourism.

  7. Contributes to research and biodiversity conservation.
