EFFECT OF WEED CONTROL ON VEGETATION AND SOIL


  1. Weed control allows crops to get necessary nutrients, water, and sunlight.

  2. Controlling weeds helps crops grow better, leading to higher yields.

  3. Weed control keeps the soil in place, preventing erosion.

  4. Controlling weeds improves airflow to plant roots, promoting better growth.

  5. Weed control reduces pests and diseases that affect crops.

  6. Weed control allows desirable plants to grow and thrive.

  7. Controlling weeds helps retain moisture in the soil for crops.
