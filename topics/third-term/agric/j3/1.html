EXPORT PROMOTION IN AGRICULTURE



Export promotion in agriculture involves activities and strategies to help farmers and agricultural businesses sell their products in other countries.





REASONS FOR EXPORT PROMOTION


  1. to increase foreign exchange earnings thereby Boosts national income.

  2. to encourage industrial growth by Expanding production capacity.

  3. to diversify the economy, thereby reducing reliance on limited sectors.

  4. to create employment opportunities, which will Stimulates job creation.

  5. to enhance competitiveness, this will Strengthens global market presence.

  6. attracting foreign investments,which also Encourages partnerships.
