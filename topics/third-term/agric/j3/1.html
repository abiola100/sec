EXPORT PROMOTION IN AGRICULTURE

Export promotion in agriculture involves activities and strategies to help farmers and agricultural businesses sell their products in other countries.



REASONS FOR EXPORT PROMOTION
  1. Finding Markets: Research which countries want our agricultural products and make deals to reduce trade barriers.

  2. Improving Quality: Ensure our products meet international standards and are of high quality.

  3. Financial and Training Support: Provide money and training to help farmers and exporters sell abroad.

  4. Marketing and Promotion: Advertise and promote our products in other countries.

  5. Efficient Logistics: Improve transportation and storage to deliver products fresh and on time.
