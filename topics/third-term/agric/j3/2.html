NIGERIAN EXPORT PEODUCE
  1. Cocoa: One of Nigeria's top export products, used in chocolate and other confectioneries.

  2. Sesame Seeds: Widely exported for use in food products and oil extraction.

  3. Cashew Nuts: Exported in large quantities, both raw and processed.

  4. Palm Oil: Used in food, cosmetics, and industrial applications.

  5. Rubber: Natural rubber for manufacturing and industrial use.

  6. Ginger: Valued for its culinary and medicinal properties.

  7. Yam: Nigeria is one of the largest producers of yam, exporting to various countries.

  8. Shea Butter: Used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food products.

  9. Soybeans: Exported for use in food products and animal feed.

  10. Hibiscus: Exported for use in teas, beverages, and food coloring.
