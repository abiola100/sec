TRANSFORMATION OF ENERGY
  1. Chemical to Mechanical: The energy in food (chemical energy) is converted into mechanical energy when we move our muscles.

  2. Electrical to Thermal: An electric heater converts electrical energy into thermal energy to warm a room.

  3. Mechanical to Electrical: A wind turbine converts the mechanical energy of spinning blades into electrical energy.

  4. Chemical to Electrical: Batteries convert chemical energy into electrical energy to power devices like flashlights and phones.

  5. Solar to Electrical: Solar panels convert radiant energy from the sun into electrical energy.

  6. Nuclear to Electrical: Nuclear power plants convert nuclear energy into thermal energy, which is then used to produce electrical energy.

  7. Mechanical to Sound: A guitar string converts mechanical energy (plucking the string) into sound energy.
