ECONOMIC IMPORTANCE OF SOLID MINERAL


  1. Revenue Generation: Export earnings and taxation from mineral exports boost government revenue.

  2. Employment Creation: Mining operations provide direct and indirect job opportunities.

  3. Industrial Development: Minerals supply raw materials for industries, supporting manufacturing and construction.

  4. Infrastructure Development: Mining investments lead to improved infrastructure like roads and power supply.

  5. Technological Advancements: Mining drives innovation and the development of a skilled workforce.

  6. Economic Diversification: Solid minerals help diversify the economy and reduce reliance on oil.

  7. Foreign Direct Investment (FDI): A regulated mining sector attracts foreign investment and joint ventures.

  8. Community Development: Mining companies invest in local projects and create business opportunities.

  9. Environmental Management: Responsible mining includes rehabilitation and sustainable land use practices.
