DRUG AND SUBSTANCE ABUSE




Drug abuse can be define as The use of drugs in a way that is harmful to oneself or others, typically involving excessive or inappropriate use.

Examples: Taking larger doses of prescription medication than prescribed, using drugs for non-medical purposes, or using illegal drugs like heroin or cocaine.





EFFECT OF DRUG ABUSE
  1. Physical Health: Feeling sick, accidents, overdosing. Heart problems, liver damage, lung issues, getting diseases .

  2. Mental Health: Confusion, anxiety, seeing things that aren't there, Depression, anxiety, memory problems.

  3. Family Relationships: Fights and problems with family members.

  4. Work/School Performance: Doing badly at work or school, missing days, getting fired, or dropping out.

  5. Legal Issues: Getting arrested for drug use or selling drugs, paying fines and legal fees, possible jail time.

  6. Financial Problems: Spending a lot of money on drugs, going into debt, and having money issues.
