DRUG AND SUBSTANCE ABUSE




Drug abuse can be define as The use of drugs in a way that is harmful to oneself or others, typically involving excessive or inappropriate use.

Examples: Taking larger doses of prescription medication than prescribed, using drugs for non-medical purposes, or using illegal drugs like heroin or cocaine.





EFFECT OF DRUG ABUSE
  1. Drug abuse can cause damage to vital organs like the liver, heart, and brain.

  2. It can lead to addiction, making it difficult to stop using the drug.

  3. Drug abuse can cause confusion, depression, and anxiety.

  4. It affects judgment, leading to risky behavior.

  5. Drug abuse can lead to relationship issues, social isolation, and conflict with others.

  6. Using illegal drugs can result in arrests and legal consequences.
