CARE FOR MAGNETS

  1. Avoid High Temperatures: Keep magnets away from high heat, as extreme temperatures can demagnetize them.

  2. Prevent Physical Damage: Handle magnets carefully to avoid dropping or striking them, as physical impacts can weaken their magnetic strength.

  3. Store Properly: Store magnets with keepers or spacers between them to avoid attracting or repelling forces that can weaken them over time.

  4. Keep Away from Electronics: Keep magnets away from electronic devices and magnetic storage media, as strong magnetic fields can damage or erase data.

  5. Avoid Corrosion: Protect magnets from moisture and corrosive environments, as rust can deteriorate their magnetic properties; use coatings or keep them dry.

  6. Keep Clean: Regularly clean magnets to remove dust and debris that can interfere with their magnetic strength and performance.
