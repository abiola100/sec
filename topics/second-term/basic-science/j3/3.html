MISUSE OF HERBAL MEDICINE

  1. Wrong Amount: Taking too much or too little can either make the medicine not work or cause harm.

  2. Self-Treatment: Using herbal medicine without a doctor's advice can lead to treating the wrong problem.

  3. Drug Interactions: Herbal medicines can mix badly with other medications, making them less effective or causing side effects.

  4. Contamination: Some herbal products might have harmful chemicals or be mixed with other drugs.

  5. Inconsistent Products: Herbal medicines can vary a lot in strength and quality.

  6. Allergies: Some people might be allergic to certain herbs.

  7. Delaying Proper Treatment: Using herbal medicine for serious conditions can delay getting the right medical treatment.


SAFETY USAGE OF HERBAL MEDICINE
  1. Talk to a Doctor: Always check with a healthcare professional before using any herbal medicine.

  2. Learn About the Herb: Understand what the herb does, its benefits, and possible side effects.

  3. Buy from Trusted Sources: Get herbal products from reliable places to ensure they are safe.

  4. Follow Instructions: Use the right amount as advised by your doctor or the product label.

  5. Watch for Reactions: Pay attention to any negative effects and tell your doctor if they occur.
