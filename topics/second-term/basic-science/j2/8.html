FACTORS THAT AFFECT THE EVAPORATION
  1. Warmer temperatures make water evaporate quicker.

  2. More exposed water (like a big puddle) evaporates faster than a small one.

  3. Dry air helps water evaporate faster. When the air is already humid, it slows down evaporation.

  4. A breeze or wind blows away the water vapor, allowing more water to evaporate.

  5. Lower air pressure makes it easier for water to evaporate.

  6. Some liquids, like alcohol, evaporate faster than others, like water.

  7. Things like salt in water can slow down evaporation.

  8. Direct heat, like from the sun, speeds up evaporation
