RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN WORK,ENERGY, AND POWER

Work and Energy:
  • Work done on an object results in a change in the object's energy.
  • For example, if work is done to lift an object, the object gains potential energy.


Energy and Power :
  • Power is the rate at which energy is transferred or converted.
  • For instance, a 60-Watt light bulb converts 60 Joules of electrical energy into light and heat every second.




Work and Power:
  • Power can be defined as the work done per unit time.
  • If a machine does 1000 Joules of work in 10 seconds,
    its power output is 1000J 10S = 100W
