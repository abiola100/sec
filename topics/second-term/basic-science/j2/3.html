TYPES OF ENERGY




  1. Kinetic Energy: Energy of motion.

  2. Potential Energy: Stored energy due to position or state.

  3. Thermal Energy: Energy related to the temperature of an object.

  4. Chemical Energy: Energy stored in chemical bonds.

  5. Electrical Energy: Energy from electric currents.

  6. Nuclear Energy: Energy released during nuclear reactions.

  7. Radiant Energy: Energy carried by light and electromagnetic waves.

  8. Mechanical Energy: Sum of kinetic and potential energy in a system.

  9. Sound Energy: Energy carried by sound waves.

  10. Gravitational Energy: Energy due to an object's position in a gravitational field.

  11. Elastic Energy: Energy stored in stretched or compressed objects.

  12. Magnetic Energy: Energy in magnetic fields.
