WORK,ENERGY, AND POWER




Work

Work is done when a force moves an object in the direction of the force.


Formula for work :

where w = work, f =force, d = Distance, Œ∏ = Angle between force and motion direction.


Types of Work


  1. Positive work (force and motion in the same direction).

  2. Negative work (force opposes motion).



Energy


Energy is the ability to do work or cause changes.


Types of Energy


  1. Kinetic energy (due to motion).

  2. Potential energy (due to position).

  3. Heat energy.

  4. Chemical energy.

  5. Electrical energy.




power

Power is the rate at which work is done or energy is used.


formula for power

power: P = WT

Where p = power, w =work, t= time.



Types of Power
  1. Mechanical power.

  2. Electrical power.

  3. Hydraulic power.
