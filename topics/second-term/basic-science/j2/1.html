WORK,ENERGY, AND POWER
Work

Work: is done when a force applied to an object moves that object.

Energy

Energy : is the capacity to do work.

Power

Power : is the rate at which work is done or energy is transferred.

CALCULATION FOR WORK
Work Formula :   W= F × d × cos (θ)
  • W = work

  • F = force

  • d = displacement

  • θ = angle between the force and displacement direction
    Example: A force of 10 N is applied to move a box 5 meters horizontally.

  • Formula: W = F × d × cos(θ)

      • F = 10 N (force)

      d = 5 m (displacement)

      θ = 0° (angle between force and displacement direction)

    Solution:

      W = 10 × 5 × cos(0°)

      W = 10 × 5 × 1

      W = 50J

      Result: The work done is 50 Joules.
