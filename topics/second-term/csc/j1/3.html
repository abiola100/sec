RULES AND REGULATION OF COMPUTER ROOM/LABORATORY


  1. No smoking, drinking, or eating is allowed in the computer room to maintain cleanliness and protect equipment.

  2. Switch off computers when they are not in use to save energy and prolong their lifespan.

  3. Keep computers out of direct heat or sunlight, as exposure can damage components.

  4. Cover computers with dust covers when not being used to prevent dust accumulation.

  5. Unplug computers if they will not be used for an extended period to avoid unnecessary power consumption.

  6. Keep the room tidy by disposing of waste properly to maintain a clean and organized environment.

  7. Use computers only for their intended purpose and follow all lab instructions to ensure smooth operations.
