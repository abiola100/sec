SAFETY MEASURE IN USING COMPUTER


meaning of safety measure


Safety measure can be defined as the steps or actions we take to protect ourselves and things around us from harm or danger.


While :


Safety measures in using computers can be defined as the actions taken to protect both the user and the computer from harm, damage, or danger while using the computer.


COMMON SAFETY MEASUERES IN USING COMPUTER


  1. Protect the computer from dust and water.

  2. Use strong and secret passwords.

  3. Do not open unknown emails or files.

  4. Unplug the computer when not in use.

  5. Keep food and drinks away from the computer.

  6. Use antivirus software to protect against viruses.

  7. Avoid clicking on suspicious links on the internet.

  8. Keep the computer dust-free by cleaning it regularly

  9. Always keep liquids like water and juice away from the computer to avoid damage.
