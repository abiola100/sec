PACKAGING OF AGRICULTURAL PRODUCT
  1. Primary Packaging: Direct contact packaging like bags, boxes, and crates.

  2. Secondary Packaging: Aggregates primary packages, such as cartons and trays.

  3. Tertiary Packaging: Facilitates bulk handling and shipping, e.g., pallets, shrink wrap.

  4. Plastic: Used for flexibility, durability, and moisture resistance.

  5. Glass: Ideal for liquids and high-value products with excellent barrier properties.

  6. Metal: Common for canned goods, offering long shelf life and protection.

  7. Paper/Cardboard: Eco-friendly and versatile for dry products and secondary packaging.

  8. Biodegradable Materials: Popular for sustainable packaging solutions.

  9. Vacuum Packaging: Removes air to prevent oxidation and spoilage.
