AGRICULTURE IN STOCK EXCHANGE

Meaning of stock exchange

A stock exchange is a marketplace where stocks (shares of ownership in businesses) and other securities are bought and sold.



STOCK BUYING AND SELLING

Stock Buying

Stock buying is the process of purchasing shares of ownership in a company through a stock exchange.


Stock selling

Stock selling is the process of transferring ownership of shares in a company to another party in exchange for money through a stock exchange.
