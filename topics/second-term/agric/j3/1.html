SALES AND PURCHASES JOURNAL


Sales journal:

A sales journal is a specialized accounting record that tracks all sales transactions of a business. It typically includes details such as the date of sale, customer name or account number, invoice number, items sold, quantities, prices, and total amounts.

    example:

    Date: January 5, 2024
    Customer: ABC Company
    Invoice Number: 001
    Items Sold: 10 units of Product A
    Price per Unit: #50
    Total Amount: #500


Purchases journal

A purchases journal is a specialized accounting record used to track all purchases of goods or services made by a business.
