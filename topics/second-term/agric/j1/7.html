USES OF FARM ANIMALS

  1. Food production: Farm animals such as cows, pigs, chickens, and goats are raised for meat, dairy, and egg production.



  2. Work and transportation: Animals like horses, oxen, and donkeys are used for plowing fields, pulling carts, and other agricultural tasks.



  3. Fertilizer production: Animal manure is used as a natural fertilizer to improve soil fertility.


  4. Wool and fiber production: Sheep and alpacas are raised for their wool, which is used to make clothing and textiles.



  5. Companionship: Some farm animals, such as horses and dogs, also serve as companions and provide emotional support to farmers and ranchers.
