MAMMAS AND NON MAMMAS FARM ANIMALS
  1. A mammal : is an animal with warm blood and fur that gives birth to live babies and feeds them milk. Examples include cows, pigs, and sheep.



  2. Non-mammal farm animals : are animals that lay eggs or have scales instead of fur. Examples include chickens (birds) and fish in farm ponds.
