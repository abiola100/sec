EFFECT OF DISEASE ON FARM ANIMAL


  1. Diseases can decrease the growth rate, milk production, egg laying, and overall performance of farm animals.

  2. Severe diseases can lead to high death rates in livestock, causing a loss of animals and income.

  3. Farmers may face financial difficulties due to the costs of treating sick animals and lost profits from reduced productivity.

  4. Animals affected by diseases may produce lower-quality meat, milk, or eggs, affecting market value.

  5. Some diseases are contagious and can quickly spread to healthy animals, leading to further losses.

  6. Treating and managing diseases can lead to higher costs for medical care, vaccinations, and other veterinary services.

  7. Disease outbreaks in farm animals can reduce consumer confidence and lower demand for animal products.

  8. Animals affected by diseases may have reduced fertility or may not be suitable for breeding, affecting future generations.
