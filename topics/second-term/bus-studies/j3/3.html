BANNING CHEMICALS




Banning chemicals refers to the prohibition of certain substances or compounds due to their potential harm to human health or the environment.





REASON FOR BANNING CHEMICALS


  1. Some chemicals can cause serious illnesses.

  2. Chemicals can damage nature and wildlife.

  3. Some chemicals stay in the environment for a long time.

  4. Certain chemicals can build up in animals, harming them and those that eat them.

  5. Banning harmful chemicals keeps people safe.

  6. Many places have laws to limit dangerous chemicals.

  7. Reducing harmful chemicals helps find safer options.
