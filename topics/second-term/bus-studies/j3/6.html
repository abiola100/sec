MODESTY

Modesty can be defined as the quality of being humble, reserved, and not seeking attention in behavior, dress, or attitude.




ATTRIBUTE OF MODESTY
  1. Humility: Staying humble and not showing off.

  2. Simplicity: Keeping things plain and straightforward.

  3. Self-control: Managing desires and actions wisely.

  4. Respectfulness: Showing regard for others in actions and words.

  5. Decency: Dressing and behaving appropriately.



EFFECT OF LIVING MODESTY

  1. You feel happier with what you have.

  2. Being humble helps you connect with others.

  3. A simpler life lowers anxiety and pressure.

  4. Thinking carefully leads to better decisions.

  5. Managing desires helps create healthy habits.



THE LINKING BETWEEN MODESTY AND EXTRAVAGANCE


  1. Modesty is about simplicity, while extravagance is about excess and showing off.

  2. Modest people prefer basic, practical things, while extravagant people want luxury items.

  3. Modesty values being content and grateful, while extravagance seeks attention and status.

  4. Modesty can build community and friendship, while extravagance may cause jealousy or competition.

  5. Living modestly can bring peace of mind, while extravagance can create stress and financial problems.
